About Us

Mtariri Funeral Services (South Africa) established in 2024, is committed to providing compassionate and reliable funeral services to the Zimbabwean community in South Africa. In our dedication to serving you, we offer tailored repatriation solutions to Zimbabwe, ensuring your loved ones can return home with dignity and respect in their final journey. Our comprehensive plans are designed to meet your specific needs, offering customizable options for individuals and families alike. We understand the importance of affordability and transparency during difficult times, which is why our pricing is fair and straightforward. With a widespread network of compassionate professionals across all provinces in South Africa, Mtariri Funeral Services is here to support you every step of the way, providing comfort and assistance when you need it most.
